================ BICEP CURL ================

Elbows moving forward | KP: Shoulder(5/6), Elbow(7/8) | Condition: Δx(elbow, shoulder) increases | Feedback: Keep elbows fixed close to torso
Elbows flaring outward | KP: Shoulder, Elbow | Condition: |Δx(elbow, shoulder)| > threshold | Feedback: Tuck elbows inward
Incomplete extension | KP: Shoulder, Elbow, Wrist | Condition: angle(shoulder, elbow, wrist) < 160° at bottom | Feedback: Fully extend arms at bottom
Incomplete contraction | KP: Shoulder, Wrist | Condition: dist(wrist, shoulder) not minimal at top | Feedback: Curl fully and squeeze at top
Leaning back / swinging | KP: Shoulder, Hip | Condition: Δx(shoulder, hip) changes over time | Feedback: Keep torso stable, avoid swinging
Uneven reps | KP: L/R Shoulder, Elbow, Wrist | Condition: angle(L arm) ≠ angle(R arm) | Feedback: Maintain symmetry between arms
Using momentum | KP: Wrist | Condition: high velocity spikes | Feedback: Slow down, use controlled movement


================ CRUNCHES ================

Excessive neck flexion | KP: Nose, Shoulder, Hip | Condition: angle(nose, shoulder, hip) too small | Feedback: Keep neck neutral, avoid pulling head
Lower back lifting | KP: Hip, Shoulder | Condition: sudden Δy(hip) increase | Feedback: Keep lower back pressed down
Using momentum | KP: Shoulder | Condition: high acceleration | Feedback: Lift slowly using abs
Incomplete lift | KP: Shoulder, Hip, Knee | Condition: insufficient angle reduction | Feedback: Lift upper body fully


================ KNEE RAISE ================

Leaning backward | KP: Shoulder, Hip | Condition: Δx(shoulder, hip) increases | Feedback: Keep torso upright

Incomplete knee height | KP: Knee, Hip | Condition: knee_y > hip_y not reached | Feedback: Raise knees higher
Uneven raise | KP: L/R Knee | Condition: Δy(L knee, R knee) | Feedback: Lift both legs evenly

Incomplete depth | KP: Shoulder, Elbow | Condition: elbow angle not < ~90° | Feedback: Lower chest closer to ground
Incomplete lockout | KP: Shoulder, Elbow, Wrist | Condition: angle < ~170° at top | Feedback: Fully extend arms at top
Head dropping | KP: Nose, Shoulder | Condition: nose_y << shoulder_y | Feedback: Keep head neutral with spine
Uneven push | KP: L/R Shoulder, Elbow, Wrist | Condition: angle/height mismatch | Feedback: Push evenly with both arms
